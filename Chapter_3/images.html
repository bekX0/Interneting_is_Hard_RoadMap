Images

Some image formats:

  • JPG
  • GIF
  • PNG
  • SVG

JPG Images

They don't allow for transparent pixels!

JPG images are good for photos.

GIF Images

Limited color palette. Don't use any other purpose but animaton

GIFs are good for animations

PNG Images

Don't have color palette limitations. Excellent fit for icons, technical diagrams, logos, etc.

PNGs are good for diagrams and icons

SVG Images

It is a vector-based graphics format, meaning it can scale up or down to any dimension without loss of quality

SVGs are amazing
